About Me

Hi, my name is Mindy and I promise I'll get to the Yellowstone part of my website, but first a little about why I've created this website. I've taken beginning courses in Python, C#, Javascript, HTML, CSS, and MS SQL. I haven't used them so I lost them, except for MS SQL. When I was learning SQL, I created a database for the 2000+ books I own and lend out. I can still write SQL queries today.

Now I'm taking a course through Coursera at the University of Michigan. It's called "Web Design for Everybody: Basics of Web Development & Coding" and includes HTML5, CSS3, Advanced Styling with Responsive Design, and Interactivity with JavaScript. I'm also learning git and github along the way. This website will help me retain the knowledge I gain. Also, creating a website it fun, so there's that.

Why Yellowstone? It's the most freakin' awesome place out there! I once had a disgruntled 10 year old ask me what was better, Disneyland or Yellowstone. I told him 'Yellowstone because that's where we are and Disneyland isn't better if we aren't there.'. I grew up at Disneyland and it's a great place, but you can't beat Yellowstone. And here we are with yet another Yellowstone website.
