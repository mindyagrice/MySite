Elk

Elk are so beautiful and majestic. When we first visited Yellowstone, right after the reintroduction of wolves, there were a lot of elk there. Not so much now that the wolves have thrived and there are multiple wolf packs.

If you visit Yellowstone in September it's mating season and you can hear the elk bugling. It's an amazing sound!
